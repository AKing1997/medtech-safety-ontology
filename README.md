# MedTech Safety & Traceability Ontology (EUDAMED-MDR)

## Overview

A semantic model designed to ensure the end-to-end traceability of medical devices across the EU market. It integrates regulatory classifications (MDR/IVDR), adverse incident reporting, clinical risk assessments, and field safety corrective actions (FSCAs) into a unified knowledge graph.

## Domain & Scope

This ontology provides a structured representation of the EUDAMED ecosystem, linking:

- **Manufacturers** (`foaf:Organization`)
- **Medical Devices** (Active, Implantable, IVD, etc.)
- **Clinical Risks** (IMDRF coding system)
- **Adverse Incidents**
- **Field Safety Corrective Actions (FSCA)**
